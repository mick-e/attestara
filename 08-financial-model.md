# AgentClear — Financial Model

---

**Important:** This is a planning model based on assumptions. All figures are estimates to be validated against market data, legal advice, and investor feedback. Actual results will differ.

## 1. Revenue Streams

Four revenue streams, activated in sequence:

| Stream | Activation | Description |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| 1. Enterprise Pilot Fees | Month 8 | Paid 90-day PoC engagements |
| 2. Managed Prover Service | Month 12 | SaaS, per-session pricing |
| 3. Enterprise SDK Licences | Month 14 | Annual subscription |
| 4. Governance Token (DAO) | Month 24+ | Phase 2, post-DAO launch |
| 5. Littledata Integration | Month 10 | Bundled upsell via Littledata |

*Note: Governance token revenue is excluded from the primary financial model due to regulatory uncertainty. It is treated as upside only.*

## 5. Funding Requirements

### Seed Round Target: €400,000–€600,000

**Use of funds:**
| Category | Amount | % |
|----------|--------|---|
| Product development (ZK circuits, SDK, smart contracts) | €180,000 | 36% |
| Legal (entity formation, IP, enforceability opinion, audits) | €80,000 | 16% |
| Go-to-market (pilot outreach, events, content) | €60,000 | 12% |
| Infrastructure and operations | €40,000 | 8% |
| Working capital / runway buffer | €140,000 | 28% |

**Runway:** €500,000 seed provides approximately 24 months runway at the Year 1-2 burn rate, reaching the point at which the business is approaching break-even.

**Target raise timeline:** Q3 2026 (following ZK PoC completion and first pilot letter of intent)

### Key Metrics for Series A Readiness (Month 24)
- ARR ≥ €300,000
- 15+ paying enterprise customers
- Gross revenue retention ≥ 90%
- 2+ published case studies with named customers
- Protocol adopted by at least one standards body working group
- Smart contract audit completed

## 6. Sensitivity Analysis

### Downside Case (50% of base case conversion)

| | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|-|--------|--------|--------|
| Revenue | €35,000 | €175,000 | €385,000 |
| Costs | €207,600 | €350,000 | €520,000 |
| EBITDA | (€172,600) | (€175,000) | (€135,000) |

In the downside case, €600,000 seed does not provide sufficient runway. Would require either reducing headcount (delay Engineer 1/2 hiring) or raising a bridge round at Month 18.

### Upside Case (150% of base case — SI partnership activates early)

| | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|-|--------|--------|--------|
| Revenue | €100,000 | €600,000 | €1,500,000 |
| Costs | €207,600 | €420,000 | €680,000 |
| EBITDA | (€107,600) | €180,000 | €820,000 |

In the upside case, break-even occurs in Year 2 Q4. Series A from a position of strength.

### Key Risk Factors

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| Sales cycles longer than modelled | High | Medium | Pilot-first approach reduces barrier |
| Smart contract audit delays launch | Medium | Medium | Begin audit process at Month 6 |
| Google A2A expands into trust layer | High | Low (12-18 months) | Protocol standard positioning |
| Legal opinion unfavourable on enforceability | High | Low-Medium | Early legal engagement |
| ZK PoC fails performance thresholds | High | Low | Alternative proof systems identified |
